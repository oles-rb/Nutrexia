2. Information We Collect

"Personal Data" refers to information that can identify you, either alone or in combination with other information. We collect the following types of data:
• Profile Data: Information you provide during setup, such as age, gender, height, weight, and wellness goals.
• Meal and Wellness Data: Meal photos, food logs, and other fitness and wellness data you input into the app.
• Technical and Usage Data: App settings, preferences, feature usage (e.g., number of food scans), and anonymized crash reports and performance data

3. How and Why We Use Your Data

How and Why We Use Your Data:
• For Performance of the Service: To fulfill our contractual obligations to you. This includes: providing personalized nutrition insights; analyzing your food using AI services; tracking your progress and recommending goals; enabling secure data backups to your personal cloud storage (with your explicit permission).
• For Legitimate Interests: To improve app performance, usability, and develop new features; To communicate with you regarding important Service-related notices, support requests, or other inquiries
• With Your Consent: We process sensitive data, such as your Fitness and Wellness Data, on the basis of your prior consent.
We do not sell your personal data or use it for targeted advertising.

4. Data Storage and International Transfers

• Data Storage: Your data is stored in the following locations: locally on your device; encrypted in your personal Google Drive or other cloud service (only if you enable the backup feature);
We do not store your personal data permanently on our own servers.
• International Transfers: Our services are hosted on servers which may be located globally, including in the United States. When your data is processed (e.g., for AI image analysis), it may be transferred to a country outside of your residence. By using our Services, you consent to this transfer, storage, and processing.

We do not sell or trade your personal data. We only share limited data with the following third parties under strict conditions:

• Legal Authorities: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
